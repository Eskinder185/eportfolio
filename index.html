About Me

I’m a passionate technologist with a strong focus on cloud computing, security, and more recently AI. Over the past few years, I’ve built and deployed AWS solutions that don’t just scale but also stay secure and cost-effective. I enjoy designing systems that balance performance with protection, making sure applications are reliable and safe for users.

Security isn’t just a feature to me—it’s part of the way I think about technology. From enforcing least-privilege IAM roles to automating monitoring and alerts, I like solving problems where cloud innovation meets smart security practices. My goal is to help teams move quickly without cutting corners on safety.

I’m also diving deeper into the world of AI, especially generative and agent AI. I’ve been experimenting with OpenAI prompts and tools, exploring how these technologies can be combined with cloud and security to create smarter, more adaptive systems. I believe the future of cloud isn’t just scalable—it’s intelligent and secure.

Outside of projects, I’m always exploring new tools and ideas—whether it’s testing emerging AWS services, reading about the latest in cybersecurity, or experimenting with AI agents. I enjoy sharing what I learn and connecting with others who are excited about building safer, more intelligent, and innovative systems in the cloud.
